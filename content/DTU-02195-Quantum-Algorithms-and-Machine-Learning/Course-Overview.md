## Learning objectives

- Explain how superposition and entanglement are used constructively in algorithm design.
- Explain common quantum algorithms.
- Explain variational methods, VQE, and QAOA-related approaches.
- Use quantum computing frameworks in practice.
- Develop quantum algorithms.
- Evaluate algorithm performance.
- Optimize algorithms for specific hardware platforms.
- Relate algorithms to complexity classes.
