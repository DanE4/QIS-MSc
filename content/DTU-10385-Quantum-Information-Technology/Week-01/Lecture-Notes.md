## Learning objectives

- Understand core concepts: entanglement, measurements, circuits, and measurement-based QC.
- Analyze simple photonic circuits for quantum computing.
- Explain engineering of quantum resource states using optics and solid-state emitters.
- Understand precision scaling in quantum metrology.
- Understand sensing of phases and magnetic fields using optical and color-center platforms.
- Design and analyze quantum sensing schemes.
- Explain central concepts in quantum communication, teleportation, and cryptography.
- Analyze continuous-variable cryptography and QRNG schemes.
