## Course information

- Course code: 10386
- Danish title: Eksperimentelle Teknikker i Kvanteteknologi
- Language of instruction: English
- ECTS: 5
- Schedule: June
- Location: Campus Lyngby
- Scope and form: Project-oriented experimental group work
- Duration: 3 weeks
- Assessment: Evaluation of experiments and reports
- Assessment details: Individualized group project with poster presentation and lab-book style report
- Evaluation: pass / not pass, internal examiner

- Responsible: Tobias Gehring

## Learning objectives

- Transform quantum theory elements into practical quantum technology.
- Diagnose and evaluate technical imperfections in experiments.
- Identify, design, and implement data-analysis strategies.
- Explain techniques used in a selected quantum technology setup.
- Discuss quantum-optics experiments.
- Organize and execute collaborative project work.
- Present and defend experimental work.
- Document experimental work rigorously in lab format.
